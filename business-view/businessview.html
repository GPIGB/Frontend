The YORBIN projet aims for better management of waste in york.

It's been developed since january 2020 by students at the university of York The solution has different views aimed at different Customers.

The Visitors are able to use our system to find the closest appropriate bin to them, businesses can monitor their recycling waste,

get printable QR codes and certificates, while the City Council will be able to maintain an optimal positioning of bins troughout the city,

making use of the latest machine learning technologies.
